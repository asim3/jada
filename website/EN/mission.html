Vision 2030

Vision 2030 is an economic plan that focuses on the diversification of the Saudi economy, and the development of different service sectors such as health, education, recreation and the arts, and tourism. As part of this plan, the Kingdom has designed targets to unleash the country’s entrepreneurial potential, aiming to develop a vibrant society that inspires citizens to participate fully in the economy and bring entrepreneurial ideas to life.

Economic development of the financial markets for private equity, venture capital and seed investment, is a key element in achieving these goals. Support will be provided to both existing and new Saudi financial organizations, as well as regional and international funds dedicated to Saudi SMEs.
